## [​](\#current-plans)  Current Plans

| Plan | /scrape (requests/min) | /crawl (requests/min) | /search (requests/min) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free | 10 | 1 | 5 |
| Hobby | 20 | 3 | 10 |
| Standard | 100 | 10 | 50 |
| Growth | 1000 | 50 | 500 |

|  | /crawl/status (requests/min) |
